25  /** Implements the IntrinsicParameters_CCD class.
26  *
27  * An IntrinsicParameters_CCD object stores the intrinsic parameters
28  * of a digitizing device such as a CDD or CMOS chip (or of a combination
29  * of analog camera + film scanner). Whereas the parent class was simply
30  * concerned with the output side, that is, the dimensions of the digitizable
31  * area (in pixels), this class is concerned with the dimensions of the
32  * digitizing device itself: the number of elements and their dimensions.
33  *
34  * Note that the number of elements of the device is typically not equal to
35  * that of the output (pixel values are interpolated from the measurements
36  * of sensor elements).
37  *
38  * Also note that the characteristics of the CCD device are completely
39  * independent from the type of optics mounted on the camera.
40  *
41  * @author jean-yves herve', 3D Group,
42  * Department of Computer Science and Statistics,
43  * University of Rhode Island
44  */
