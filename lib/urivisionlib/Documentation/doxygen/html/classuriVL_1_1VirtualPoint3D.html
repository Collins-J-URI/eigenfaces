Detailed Description

Implements the VirtualPoint3D class.

A VirtualPoint3D belongs to a VirtualPart3D to be embedded into a real scene for 1-n cameras. A VirtualPoint3D object stores the 3D coordinates of a point as well as information about normal directions, visibility relative ot cameras, etc.

Author
Fang Han, 3D Group, Department of Computer Science and Statistics, University of Rhode Island
